# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-10

## Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how this application (“the App”) handles user data.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Data Collection
The App itself does not directly collect, store, or process personal data entered by the user.

However, certain data may be automatically processed by Apple and third-party services that are required for core functionality.

## Game Center
The App uses Apple Game Center for authentication and gameplay-related features such as achievements and leaderboards.

Game Center data (such as player ID, achievements, and scores) is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple’s Privacy Policy.
The developer does not have access to personal information associated with the user’s Apple ID.

## Firebase Services
The App uses Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

### Firebase Analytics
Firebase Analytics is used to collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve the App.
Collected data does not personally identify users.

### Firebase Crashlytics
Firebase Crashlytics is used to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability.
This data may include device information and app state at the time of a crash but does not include personally identifiable information.

### Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database
Firebase Firestore is used to store application-related data required for functionality.
Stored data is limited to app-specific data and is not used to identify individual users.

Firebase data processing is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## In-App Purchases
The App offers In-App Purchases.
All payment processing is handled securely by Apple.
The developer does not receive or store any payment or billing information.

## Location Data
The App may request access to location data to support app functionality.
Location data is not stored or shared by the developer beyond what is required for the feature.

## Push Notifications
The App may send push notifications to provide important information or updates.
Push notification tokens are used solely for message delivery and are not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Sharing
The App does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties except as required to provide the services described above.

## Data Retention
Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality or as required by the services used.

## Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
